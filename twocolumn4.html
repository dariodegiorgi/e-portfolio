Stastics exercices

In this section a overview of the stastics exercices are explained.

During this module statistics were also covered, on the following print screen there is an example of an exercise on statistics from Unit 8.

In the first exercise on presented in Figure 1, I took statistics for the year 2021 for different antiviruses, with scores representing awards, from 1 to 3. McAfee's mean number is 2.85 and MalwareBytes is 1.14. The data shows that McAfee would be a better choice based on these numbers with a difference of 1.71 between the 2 products.

In the second exercise on figure 2, I took hypothetical data from January 2015 to March 2022 and then counted the last 5 years by different types of antiviruses: McAfee, Norton, MalwareBytes and Microsoft. On this chart we can see that MalwareBytes is in top position, with McAfee not far behind and Microsoft antivirus is in last position.
